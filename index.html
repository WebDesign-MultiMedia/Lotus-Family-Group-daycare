Our Story

Welcome to Lotus Family

At Lotus Family Group Daycare, we believe every child deserves a warm, loving, and stimulating environment where they can flourish. Under the care of Denise Capellan, we create a home-away-from-home experience for children ages 6 weeks to 13 years old.

Our program blends play-based learning with structured educational activities, ensuring that every child develops socially, emotionally, and academically at their own unique pace.
